Krillin

About me

I’m Krillin, Earth’s strongest human—well, at least I try to be. I trained under Master Roshi, fought alongside Goku against Saiyans, androids, and galactic tyrants, and somehow I’m still around to tell the story. I might not be the tallest guy on the team, but I make up for it with heart, technique, and the sharpest Destructo Disc in the universe. When I’m not saving the world, I’m either training, hanging out with my friends, or trying not to get roasted by Android 18.

My moments

Training With Goku

This was the day I first met Goku. I was trying so hard to look confident, but honestly? I had no idea who this wild kid with a tail even was. I just knew we were about to train together… and maybe compete a little.

Temple Life Is Hard

Life at the Orin Temple basically meant getting bullied nonstop. I was the smallest kid there, so everyone loved picking on me. It wasn’t fun, but it definitely pushed me to get stronger.

The Stinky Tournament Guy

This was from my very first martial arts tournament. They put me up against a guy whose special ability was… being insanely stinky. Not exactly the epic showdown I imagined, but hey—training is training.

Peace Out

Sometimes you just gotta throw up the peace sign and pretend everything’s under control. Spoiler: it usually wasn’t. But at least I looked confident.

Master Roshi’s ‘Training Materials’

Master Roshi sent me out to buy… uh… ‘magazines.’ I was too young to understand why he needed them, but old enough to know he definitely shouldn’t have.

Trust Issues

Yeah, even as a baby I had this face. I wasn’t impressed by anyone or anything. Honestly, I still make this expression every time Goku explains a plan.
